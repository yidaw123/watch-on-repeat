How to Loop & Repeat YouTube Videos

Ever found a song, an ambient track, or a funny clip that you just want to play over and over again? Endlessly repeating YouTube videos is incredibly simple with Watch On Repeat.

Step 1: Paste Your Link

Simply copy the URL of the YouTube, Vimeo, or Twitch video you want to watch. Paste it into the search bar at the top of our app and hit "Loop It". That's it! Your video will now play continuously, automatically restarting when it reaches the end.

Pro-Tip: The URL Trick

You can instantly loop any video without copying and pasting! Just go to your browser's address bar and replace youtube, vimeo, or dailymotion with watchonrepeat. (For Twitch VODs, change twitch.tv to watchonrepeat.com).

Example:
Change: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
To: https://www.watchonrepeat.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

How to Loop a Specific Part of a Video (A/B Looping)

Sometimes you don't want to loop the whole video — you just want to repeat a specific 10-second section. This is called A/B looping.

  • Load your video into the player.
  • Below the video, click the Start (A) button when the part you want begins.
  • Click the End (B) button when that part finishes.
  • Toggle the loop switch on! The video will now endlessly repeat just that specific segment.

Pro-Tip: The Bookmarklet

Want to loop videos even faster? Drag our "Loop on WOR" bookmarklet from the app's sidebar directly to your browser's bookmarks bar. The next time you are on YouTube and want to repeat a video, click the bookmark, and it will instantly open here!
