The Best YouTube Study Tool & Video Note-Taker

YouTube is packed with incredible educational content, from university lectures to coding tutorials. But passively watching a video isn't enough to retain the information. Watch On Repeat transforms passive watching into an interactive study session.

Timestamped Note-Taking

Throw away the messy paper notes that lose their context. Our built-in Notes dashboard allows you to type notes right alongside the video. Every time you press enter, your note is automatically tagged with the exact timestamp of the video. When reviewing for an exam, simply click the timestamp to instantly jump back to that exact moment in the lecture.

Loop Complex Concepts

Did the professor explain a complex math equation too quickly? Use the A/B looper to isolate that specific 30-second explanation. Let it repeat while you process the information and take detailed notes, without having to constantly hit rewind.

Cloud Sync Your Study Materials

Create a free account to automatically save your notes and playlists to the cloud. You can study on your laptop in the library, and review your notes on your phone on the bus ride home.
