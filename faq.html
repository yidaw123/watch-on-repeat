Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Audio Recorder work?

Our audio recorder lets you record your microphone while the video plays, which is perfect for musicians practicing covers or language learners practicing pronunciation. The audio is stored locally in your browser for privacy and disappears when you refresh the page. You can download your recordings from the "Recorded Audio" tab before leaving.

Is there a URL shortcut trick?

Yes! You don't have to copy and paste. Whenever you're on a YouTube video, just change youtube.com to watchonrepeat.com in your browser's address bar and hit Enter. The video will instantly load in our looper.

What are Multi-Segments?

Unlike basic loopers that only let you loop one section of a video, Watch On Repeat Premium and Pro allow you to save multiple loop segments for a single video. This is extremely useful for breaking down a guitar solo into chunks or practicing different sections of a dance routine.

Why is the Playback Speed dropdown disabled for some platforms?

The official Embedded Player APIs for Twitch, Facebook, SoundCloud, Mixcloud, and Loom do not currently support external playback speed controls. Because of these restrictions, third-party sites like ours cannot programmatically speed up or slow down media from those platforms. Playback speed controls are fully supported for YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, and your own uploaded local files.

How does "Upload Your Own File" work?

Our local file player allows you to open videos directly from your computer without uploading them to the internet, guaranteeing 100% privacy. You can use all of our core features on your local files, including creating multi-segment A/B loops, changing playback speeds, and writing time-stamped notes. Your loops and notes are saved directly to your browser's local storage and are automatically reloaded the next time you open the same file. We currently support standard web video formats: .mp4, .webm, and .ogg.

Is WatchOnRepeat free to use?

Yes, our core looping and note-taking features are 100% free to use forever.

Does Premium remove all ads?

Upgrading to Premium removes all WatchOnRepeat banner and site ads, creating a beautiful distraction-free interface. You also unlock exclusive functions like multiple segment loops, advanced loop control, customizable hotkeys, and more. (Please note that we cannot block YouTube's embedded pre-roll video ads).
