Pro Controls Guide

Start / End Time Fine Tune (- / +)

Nudges your loop's start or end time by tiny fractions of a second (0.05s) so you can get the timing absolutely perfect.

Loop Segment Shift (<- / ->)

Shifts the entire loop window backwards or forwards without changing its total length. Great for moving your loop to the next bar of a song.

Loop Window Multiplier (½x / 2x)

Instantly cuts your loop duration in half, or doubles it. Perfect for breaking down tricky sections into smaller pieces.

Gradual Tempo (Auto-increase)

Automatically increases the playback speed slightly (+0.05x) every time the video loops, helping you build up your speed naturally.
