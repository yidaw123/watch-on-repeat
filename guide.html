How to Use WatchOnRepeat: Step-by-Step Guide

Welcome to WatchOnRepeat! Whether you're learning a new guitar solo, practicing a dance routine, or studying for an exam, this guide will show you exactly how to use our powerful video looper.

Step 1: Load a Video

You can load any video from YouTube, Vimeo, or Twitch. Simply paste the URL into the search bar at the top of the app and press Enter. The video will load instantly.

[Screenshot: The main search bar showing a pasted YouTube URL]

Step 2: Set Your A/B Loop Points

Once your video is playing, click the Set Start (A) button when you reach the beginning of the section you want to practice. Then, click Set End (B) when you reach the end of the section.

The video will now seamlessly loop between those two points forever!

[Screenshot: The A and B buttons highlighted on the control bar]

Step 3: Fine-Tune Your Loop

Did you miss the exact start of the guitar riff by a split second? No problem! Use the Fine-tune (+/-) buttons to nudge the start or end points backward or forward by fractions of a second.

[Screenshot: The fine-tune controls showing milliseconds adjustments]

Step 4: Slow It Down

If the section is too fast to learn by ear, use the Speed Control to slow the video down to 0.75x, 0.5x, or even 0.25x speed. This is essential for transcribing fast solos or catching subtle dance steps.

Step 5: Use Keyboard Shortcuts

For the best experience, keep your hands on your instrument and use our keyboard shortcuts:

  • Spacebar: Play / Pause
  • [ : Set Loop Start
  • ] : Set Loop End
  • \ : Clear Loop
  • Right/Left Arrow: Seek forward/backward 5 seconds
